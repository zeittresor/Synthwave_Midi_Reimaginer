Synthwave MIDI Reimaginer GUI v0.1.5
======================================

Fixes
-----
- Seed field no longer becomes effectively unusable after playback/render workflow.
- New Seed button now remains enabled even when Auto Seed is active.
- Clicking New Seed now creates a fresh seed and switches Auto Seed off, so the visible seed is used reproducibly on the next render.
- Editing the seed field now also switches Auto Seed off automatically.
- Auto Seed still works as before: when enabled, every render chooses a fresh random seed and writes it into filename, MIDI metadata and analysis TXT.

Notes
-----
- This is mainly a GUI/UX hotfix. The MIDI generation core is unchanged from v0.1.4.
