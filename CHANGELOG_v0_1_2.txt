Synthwave MIDI Reimaginer GUI v0.1.2
======================================

Hotfix for render stage after successful analysis/MIDI generation.

Fixed:
- `NameError: name 'progress' is not defined` in `collect_notes_for_render()` when rendering WAV.
- `render_wav()` now passes the progress callback into note collection.
- Added clearer WAV render progress messages.

Known behavior:
- MP3 is still optional. If no real ffmpeg is found, the app writes MIDI/WAV and skips MP3 safely.
